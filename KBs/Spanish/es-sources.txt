;; Health and Safety
;;
;;   It is desirable to maintain the well being of a body. Some things in
;;   the world are beneficial for human health and safety. Other things are
;;   threats to human health and safety.
;;
;;   Targets are mapped to:
;;   - R#THREAT (e.g., monsters, tsunamis, diseases, parasites)
;;   - R#THREATENED (e.g., sick people, infested people, contaminated
;;     things)
;;   - R#PROTECTION or mitigation of threats (e.g., medicine, protection,
;;     shelter)
;;   - R#BENEFICIAL or necessary things (e.g., beating hearts, food, doctors)

;; A Right / Derecho
;;   That which is due to anyone by just claim, legal guarantees, moral
;;   principles, etc.
;;
;;   Conjunto de principios y normas, expresivos de una idea de justicia
;;   y de orden, que regulan las relaciones humanas en toda sociedad y cuya
;;   observancia puede ser impuesta de manera coactiva.
;;   [A body of principles and norms, expressive of an idea of justice and
;;   order, that regulate human relations in all of society and whose
;;   observance can be imposed in a coercive manner.]
;;
;;   Note: There is no mention of a right due to anyone in Spanish.


(B (name right-to/derecho-a)
   (=> (^ (S#A_RIGHT e0 :0.45) (SS#A_RIGHT%TYPE x e0 :0.45))
       (^ (derecho-nn e0 x) (a-in e1 x y ))))
(B (name inalienable-right/derecho-inalienable)
   (=> (^ (S#A_RIGHT e0 :0.45) (SS#A_RIGHT%TYPE x e0 :0.45))
       (^ (inalienable-adj e0 x) (derecho-nn e1 x ))))
